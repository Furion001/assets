NPC Interaction

NPCs, also known as 'Non-Player Characters', are AI controlled people in Master Sword. These people will sell things to you, give you quests, help you with quests you've already started, or maybe they will just try to kill you.

When you look at an NPC in the lower right hand of the screen it shows thier name and thier hostility towards you.
If it says friendly, you cannot attack them, and they will not attack you.

If it says Hostile, then you are most likely already engaged in combat with them.

Some NPCs can be quite talkitive. To talk to NPCs press your setsay key, default u, until the icon in the bottom right corner shows this image

The best way to get a responce is to say 'Hello' or 'Hail'. If the NPC is in any mood to talk they will respond.

You'll often see text [in brackets] in thier speech. This is defines a key word for further interaction.

NPCs can give you all manor of quests, from lowly errands, to slaying a dragon in a far off region. They will normally grant a reward for what you do for them, sometimes gold, sometimes magical weapons and armour.
