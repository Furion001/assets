Titles

As you go up in levels in your various stats, your class will change. Each title means something different, for example, if you excel in Archery and Blunt Arms you would be called a "Hardy Archer". Similarly, if you prefered soley Axes, you would be called a "Titan". The two highest stats are taken into consideration for your title, so long as they are within 5 levels of eachother.
