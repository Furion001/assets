Leveling Up

Master Sword's leveling system is a bit different from other RPGs. Instead of having a single expierence bar that causes you to go up in simple levels, and then distrobuting points of some kind, you train in certain weapons or abilities. The more you train in a certain field the more influence it has on your class title.
To see all of these statistics, press your playerinfo key (default p). It will bring up a window like this.

This page tells you all about your character. It has your name, class, gold ammount, as well as all of your stats. By pressing the page up and page down keys, you can look through each stat.

The stat highlighted in red is the currently selected stat. On the right are the expanded details of the current stat. Each stat, except Spell Casting, has 3 areas: Proficiency, Balance, and Power. Proficiency affects your chance to hit your target, while Power affects how much damage you do. Balance allows you to hit more often for higher damage.
