Controls

Default Controls
KeyConsole CommandDescription
ENTER
 accept Accepts actions like party joinrequests, traveling from zone to zone and accepting item/gold offers
Q use Equips a pack, bow or armor piece or sheathe a weapon
R remove Gives a list of the current items you are wearing
C drop Drops anything in your hand(s)
X
 get
 Picks up an item from the ground. If there are stacked items, you will get a list and you must choose which item to pick up
MOUSE 1 +attack Attacks with your fists, a weapon you're currently holding or drinking potions
MOUSE 2 +attack2 Attacks with your offhand Weapon
MOUSE 3 switchhands Switches your primary hand
1 quickslot weapon Cycles through weapons in your inventory
2 quickslot spell Cycles through spells you have memorized
3 inventory Bring up the inventory menu
P playerinfo Displays your race, name, statistics, player kills, experience and gold
V act List emotions and actions you can perform
Y say_text 0 Chatting on the global channel
U say_text 1 Chatting on the local channel (short range, mainly used for NPCs)
I say_text 2 Chatting on the party channel
F1-F10 +quickslot 1-10 Pulls out the item bound to that slot
F11 menu interact Brings up a menu of which allows you to intereact with items, npcs, and players
F12 menu main Brings up a menu which allows you to cast votes and form parties
<none> joinparty "name" Creates a party with the desired name. If you're the leader, you'll have to press the Accept key to let your friend join. Being in a party enabled the private party-chat
<none> leaveparty Leaves your current party. If you're the leader, the second one who joined becomes the new one

Client Variables

VariableAllowed ValuesDescription
ms_music 1/0 Switches the music on/off
ms_music_volume 0.0-1.0 Turns the volume up/down. 0.0 is silent, 1.0 is full blast
ms_txthud_history 0-100 Controls how many lines are saved in the chat box
ms_txthud_decaytime Integer The chat box will shrink by one line every seconds
ms_reflect 1/0 Enable or disable reflective surfaces (Enabled = moderate performance hit)

Key Combos

CombinationDescription
Double-tapping Forward Double tapping your forward key (W by default) makes you run. Running drains stamina
